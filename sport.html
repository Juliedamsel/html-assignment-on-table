FOOTBALL AS A SPORT

Football (also known as Soccer ) is a Team sport played between two teams of 11 players each. It is widely considered as the most popular sport in the world. A game ball,it is played on a rectangular grass field,or occasionally artificial turf, width a goal post at each end of the field. The objective of the game is to score by manoevring the ball into the opposing goal post; Only the keeper may use their hands or arm to propel the ball in general play. The team that scores the most goal by the end of the game wins. If the score is tied at the end of the game, either a draw is declared or the game goes into extended time, depending on the format of the competition.
